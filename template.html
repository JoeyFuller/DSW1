How to make: Sauceless Pepperoni Pizza

This is a short guide to pizza making for people who hate tomato sauce!

Why would you not want pizza sauce on your pizza?!

Some people like myself have acid reflux and the sauce can cause issues like heartburn!

Ingredients

  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Dough
  • Butter
  • Garlic
