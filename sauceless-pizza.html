How to make: Sauceless Pepperoni Pizza

This is a short guide to pizza making for people who hate tomato sauce!

Why would you not want pizza sauce on your pizza?!

Some people like myself have acid reflux and the sauce can cause issues like heartburn!

Ingredients

  • Pepperoni
  • 2 Lb Shredded Cheese
  • 1 Cup of Olive Oil
  • 1 Large Pizza Dough
  • 1 TBSP Butter
  • Garlic Poweder

Step-by-Step cooking instructions!

  1. Stretch out pizza dough on pizza stone or pan
  2. Melt 1 TBSP of butter and spread it along the pizza dough
  3. Next take 1 cup of olive oil and spead it evenly over the button on the dough, ensure to cover the entire dough!
  4. Then you want to sprinkle any desired amount of garlic powerder into the olive oil (Be sure to not over do it!)
  5. Almost there! Next sprinkle 1 Lb of shredded cheese over the entire pizza as evenly as possible
  6. Next place as many pepperoni as you please evenly around the pizza
  7. Then place the second pount of shredded cheese over the pepperoni!

Heating Instructions

  1. Preheat over to 425F
  2. Place pizza in the middle of the oven
  3. Allow to cook for 15 mins or until the crust is brown
  4. Allow the pizza to cool for 5 to 10 mins to avoid burning your mouth!
